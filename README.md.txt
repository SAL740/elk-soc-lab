# EC-Council CCT – 50 Sample Q&A

---

### 1. Identify the type of threat intelligence that provides information about specific threats to the organization, which includes contextual information about security events and incidents that help defenders identify potential risks.

- Operational threat intelligence
- Tactical threat intelligence
- Technical threat intelligence
- Strategic threat intelligence

**Answer:** Operational threat intelligence

---

### 2. In which of the following IH&R steps does the IH&R team eliminate the root cause of an incident and closes all the attack vectors to prevent similar incidents in future?

- Recovery
- Eradication
- Containment
- Incident triage

**Answer:** Eradication

---

### 3. Susan, a security professional, wanted to confirm the identity of newly joined employees. She sent email links of the company's portal to all the newly joined employees. The employees need to enter initial access token to login on to the portal, then they will receive an OTP on their mobile phone to upload their scanned certificate copies. Which of the following types of authentication has Susan implemented in the above scenario?

- Password authentication
- Two-factor authentication
- Biometric authentication
- Single sign-on (SSO) authentication

**Answer:** Two-factor authentication

---

### 4. Which of the following types of honeypot does not emulate anything but runs actual vulnerable services or software on production systems with a real OS and applications?

- Low-interaction honeypot
- Medium-interaction honeypot
- High-interaction honeypot
- Pure honeypot

**Answer:** High-interaction honeypot

---

### 5. Adonis, a security engineer, received a complaint from an employee stating his mobile device has been misplaced by a miscreant, and that it contains certain confidential organizational data. For this reason, Adonis used a technique that allows him to erase all the device data by remotely executing a command. Identify the technique used by Adonis in the above scenario.

- Remote wipe
- Screen lock
- Passwords and PINs
- Biometrics

**Answer:** Remote wipe

---

### 6. Which of the following individuals are known as steering committees that hold complete control over the data in an organization and are solely responsible for the data assets of the organization?

- Data owners
- Data controller
- Data processor
- Data steward

**Answer:** Data owners

---

### 7. Identify the VPN core functionality in which packets over a VPN are enclosed within another packet that has a different IP source and destination because concealing the source and destination of the packets can protect the integrity of the data sent.

- Authentication
- Encapsulation
- Encryption
- Packet filtering

**Answer:** Encapsulation

---

### 8. Bentley, a security inspector, was assigned to install a physical security control in the company premises to defend against intrusion attempts. He implemented a security control that involves motion sensors connected with video surveillance to monitor and identify illegitimate intrusion attempts. Which of the following types of physical security control has Bentley implemented in the above scenario?

- Deterrent controls
- Recovery controls
- Detective controls
- Compensating controls

**Answer:** Detective controls

---

### 9. In which of the following PKI trust models the certificate authorities do not contain any intermediate CAs or trust anchor and the clients usually depend on their local CAs, which serves as a starting point?

- Peer-to-peer trust model
- Hybrid trust model
- Bridge trust model
- Bridge trust model

**Answer:** Peer-to-peer trust model

---

### 10. In which of the following investigation phases do forensic officers perform data acquisition, preservation, and analysis of the evidentiary data to identify the source of a crime and the culprit?

- Preparatory phase
- Pre-investigation phase
- Post-investigation phase
- Investigation phase

**Answer:** Investigation phase

### 11. Mateo, a professional hacker, was recruited by an agency to steal sensitive data from a rival company. From a remote location, he discovered vulnerabilities in the target company’s network using a vulnerability scanner. He exploited them to intrude into the network and steal confidential data. Identify the threat source exploited by Mateo in the above scenario.

- External threats
- Internal threats
- Unintentional threats
- Natural threats

**Answer:** External threats

### 12. Kayson, a network engineer at an organization, was troubleshooting a network issue in the organization’s network. Which of the following network issues was Kayson trying to resolve in the above scenario?

- Network cable is unplugged
- Network path cannot be found
- Physical connectivity issues
- Local connectivity issues

**Answer:** Network path cannot be found

### 13. Which of the following mobile device connection methods is based on a single network tower that serves devices located within a specific radius and are installed in urban, suburban, & rural areas and cover a large distance?

- Cellular communication
- Near-field communication
- Infrared communication
- Wi-Fi communication

**Answer:** Cellular communication

### 14. Which of the following zones of the Purdue model is a part of IT, in which supply-chain management and scheduling are performed using business systems such as SAP and ERP?

- Enterprise zone
- Manufacturing zone
- OT systems
- Industrial demilitarized zone

**Answer:** Enterprise zone

### 15. Peter, a network administrator, was tasked with detecting outdated services that are active on the organizational network. For this purpose, he executed an nmap command that retrieves all the service versions that are currently active on the organization’s network. Identify the nmap command executed by Peter in the above scenario.

- nmap -sS <IP address>
- nmap -O <IP address>
- nmap -sT <IP address>
- nmap -sV <IP address range>

**Answer:** nmap -sV <IP address range>

### 16. Karl, a security professional at an organization, while analyzing Windows event logs identified that a threat actor made multiple attempts to gain access to the organization’s network but failed to do so, due to strong security controls deployed on the network. Identify the type of event data discussed in the above scenario.

- Error
- Warning

- Failure audit

### 17. In which of the following phases of business impact analysis (BIA) does the BIA team adopt different information-gathering methods such as interviews and questionnaire surveys to determine assets that are critical to different business functions?

- Initiation of the BIA
- Acquisition of information
- Analysis of information
- Documentation of findings

**Answer:** Acquisition of information

### 18. Which of the following techniques refers to spoofing a physical address with the physical address of a legitimate user on the network and involves sniffing a network for physical addresses of legitimate clients connected to the network?

- MAC cloning
- DHCP spoofing attack
- Domain hijacking
- MAC flooding

**Answer:** MAC cloning

***

### 19. Which of the following levels of virtualization makes virtual devices independent of the physical computer hardware and creates a massive pool of storage areas for different virtual machines running on the hardware?

- File-system virtualization
- Storage-device virtualization
- Fabric virtualization
- Server virtualization

**Answer:** Storage-device virtualization

### 20. Which of the following is an accreditation standard that guides certification bodies on the formal processes they must follow when auditing their client’s information security management systems (ISMSs) against ISO/IEC 27001 in order to certify or register them compliant?

- ISO/IEC 27005
- ISO/IEC 27006
- ISO/IEC TR 27008
- ISO/IEC 27010

### 21. Graham, a certified pen tester, was appointed by an organization to test its security posture. - Attack phase

### 22. Which of the following ciphers can resist a wide range of attacks and provides message secrecy and integrity and uses a one-way mathematical function that is capable of factoring large prime numbers?

### 29. Which of the data deidentification techniques involves the process of attaching random characters to a hashed field in a table to increase the complexity and difficulty in retrieving the hashes, providing strong defense against dictionary and rainbow table attacks?
